Healthcare Providers-CPR/BLS/AED

CPR Life Saver – Gift of Life provides certification training on life-threatening emergency and life-saving skills to healthcare providers CPR on ADULT, CHILD and INFANT and use of AED then CHOKING how to clear any foreign body airway obstruction.

BLS for all medical professionals:

  • Healthcare Providers: Like RN,LPN,CNA,PT,OT,RT,HHA, Medical/Dental Asstts..
    • Pharmacists
    • Professional rescuers
    • Lifeguards
  • CPR – Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation
  • BLS – Basic Life Support
  • AED – Automated External Defibrillators

If you are interested to get certify please register online enrollment form or call us 347-636-6370 and we will process your registration for you. Our AHA certification is valid for 2 years.
