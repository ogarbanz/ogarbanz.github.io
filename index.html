Welcome to CPR Life Saver-Gift of Life

CPR Life Saver-Gift of Life is an official Training Site of American Heart Association CPR and ECC certification programs to Healthcare Professionals like RN,LPN,CNA,PT,OT,RT,HHA, Medical/Dental Assistants, Workplace, Schools/Day Care Centers and Emergency First Responders ..etc. We follow the latest guidelines from American Heart Association . Our professional Instructors/Trainers are all certified Instructors through the American Heart Association and have experience in teaching these life saving techniques.

Our goal to train you for life saving skills that is useful in many Cardiac Emergencies such as HEART ATTACK, CARDIAC ARREST and STROKE and also useful FIRST AID emergencies. We want to equip you with the capability of saving someone’s life during these life threatening emergencies. It is always better to gain knowledge and skills to save someone’s life and doing something better than- do nothing, every second’s counts in case of emergency.

Chain of Survival is >Early Access >Early CPR > Early Defibrillation>Early Advanced Care
